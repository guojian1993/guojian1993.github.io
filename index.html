Doctor of Engineering

Tsinghua University

Biography

Jian Guo is a postdoctoral fellow at tsinghua University’s School of Civil Engineering and Hydraulic Engineering. The main research direction is the formation, evolution and movement mechanism of landslide and debris flow disaster. He conducted extensive field surveys and developed disaster dynamics simulation software.

At present, his research has been transformed from single disasters to chain disasters, focusing on the transformation and coupling effects of different disasters.
